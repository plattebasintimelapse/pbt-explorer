Explore the Platte Basin

The Platte Basin drains roughly 90,000 square miles in parts of three states. From the snow-capped peaks and alpine tundra of the Colorado Rockies to the braided river floodplain and tallgrass prairie along Nebraska's eastern border, the Platte drops nearly 12,000 feet before joining the Missouri River.

Timelapse cameras have been installed at important locations throughout this geography, taking photographs once every hour, every daylight hour of everyday.
